Microsoft 365 Virtual Marathon is a free, online, 60-hour event happening May 4-6, 2022. We will have content going the whole time with speakers from around the globe. This event is free for all wanting to attend. Join us as we talk about the many different pieces of Microsoft 365, including Azure.

We have English, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, and Spanish sessions. All English sessions(Microsoft Teams limitation) will have live captions.

Videos are being processed and will be uploaded to YouTube as we complete them. When a session is available, we'll update the link on the schedule.

We have keynotes from key Microsoft employees and sessions from thought leaders and community members from around the world.
