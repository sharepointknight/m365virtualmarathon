Microsoft 365 Virtual Marathon is an online, 36 hour event happening May 27-28 2020. We will have sessions going the whole time with speakers from around the globe. This event is free for all wanting to attend. Come join us as we talk about the many different pieces of Microsoft 365.

With all the in person conferences cancelling due to COVID-19 this is a great opportunity to connect with the community and get some great training at a great price. This is free for all attendees. We will be coordinating with sponsors to help cover expenses of running this event and for prize drawings.
